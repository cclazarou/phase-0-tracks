A Chinese Dish Recipe

This is a plain website that teaches you how to make the Simplest Chinese Dish - Tomato with Fried Egg.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Tomato
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the pan then pour in some oil.
  2. Mix the eggs well in a bowl.
  3. When oil becomes hot, turn down the fire and cook scrambled eggs.
  4. Take out eggs, pour more oil, and put in sliced tomatoes.
  5. When tomato became juicy, put eggs back in.
  6. Put salt and sugar before take out.
